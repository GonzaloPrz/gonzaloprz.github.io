About Me

I am a Biomedical Engineer graduated from Favaloro University with a Grade Point Average of 9.31/10. My undergraduate dissertation, “Dynamics of translation processes: a new approach to bilingual memory”, was awarded highest honors (10/10). Currently, I am pursuing my PhD under a prestigious doctoral grant from CONICET at the Cognitive Neuroscience Center (CNC) at San Andrés University, while simultaneously working as a Machine Learning Engineer at TELL app.

My work bridges data science, advanced audio signal processing, and cognitive neurology. I specialize in developing scalable, automated speech and language processing pipelines to identify speech biomarkers capable of detecting mild cognitive impairment (MCI) and neurodegenerative conditions.
