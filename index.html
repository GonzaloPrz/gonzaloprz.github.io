About Me

Hi! I am a Biomedical Engineer graduated from Favaloro University (GPA: 9.31/10). Currently, I am a PhD student at the Cognitive Neuroscience Center (San Andrés University) and a Machine Learning Engineer at TELL app in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

My research is focused on the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, and Cognitive Neuroscience, specifically developing automated speech biomarkers for the early detection of mild cognitive impairment and neurodegenerative disorders.
